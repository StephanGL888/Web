Norman Duke

About:

Born on March 25, 1964, Norm Duke is an active professional bowler that is currently on the Professional Bowlers Association Tour.

Major Titles:

  • 1993 - USBC Masters, Tulsa, Okla.
  • 1994 - Tournament of Champions, Akron, Ohio
  • 2000 - PBA National Championship, Toledo, Ohio
  • 2008 - Denny's World Championship, Indianapolis, Ind.
  • 65th Denny's U.S Open, North Brunswick, N.J.
  • PBA World Championship, Wicita, Kan.
  • 2011 - U.S. Open, North Brunsick, N.J.
